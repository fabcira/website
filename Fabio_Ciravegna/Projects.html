Research Projects

Current Projects

  • Active 10 a project developed on behalf of Public Health England. This is an app and server infrastructure designed to collect sub-minute tracking data from hundreds of thousands of citizens
  • SETA: A project on a​ ubiquitous data and service ecosystem for better metropolitan mobility, 3 years, €5.5M of which €1.2M for Sheffield. I am the project director (2016-2019)
  • Football Whispers: industrial project on mining of information from millions of social Web messages (2015-2016).
  • MoveMore Sheffield: monitoring individual lifestyle using mobile phones developed for Sheffield's MoveMore initiative (2015-2016).

Past Projects

  • Time to change! Using the transition from school to university to promote healthy lifestyle habits in young people. Project funded by the Medical Research Council: 3 years, £369k (2012-2014).
  • Big Energy Upgrade, ERDF funds. Our group works on providing feedback on house energy consumption based on smart meters, (2011- 2013).

Our group is fully funded by Rolls-Royce to work on knowledge and information capture during meetings (2010-2013).
  • RAnDMS (Real time Analysis of Digital Media Streams), funded by the EPSRC, 1 year, £208k (2012-2013).

  • Samulet (Strategic Affordable Manufacturing in the UK with Leading Environmental Technology). This is a £90m research project aiming to revolutionise the lifecycle of aerospace engines to address environmental emissions. We participate in Workpackage 3.7 focussing upon Semantic support for the Knowledge lifecycle. Our group is funded 100% by Rolls Royce to work on visualisation of information and knowledge about the manufacturing process. (November 2009 - February 2012).
  • SmartProducts, an Integrated project funded as part of the European Framework 7. It concerns embedding of proactive knowledge into smart products. Coordinator is SAP, users are Philips, Fiat and Eads. (February 2009 - January 2012).

  • EPSRC IRC Advanced Knowledge Technologies (AKT), a six-year multi-million pound project funded by the EPSRC research council on advanced knowledge technologies (role: co-investigator and technical manager for Sheffield) (2000-2007)
