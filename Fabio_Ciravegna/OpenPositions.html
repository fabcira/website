Open Positions

Here you can find the open positions for my group. You are very welcome to enquiry about any of them but I would be grateful if you could read these tips before contacting me.
There are two types of positions:
  • Academic Positions: these are positions as faculty
  • PhD Positions: these are positions for studying as a full time (or part-time) student. I am always very happy to receive applications from perspective students. However studying at the university costs unfortunately quite a bit of money. Students must be sure to have the required financial support. Sometimes we have available studentships. If we do, they are listed on this page. Some students instead have their own means (e.g. personal funds or grants from different institutions, e.g. national bodies, especially non-European bodies). Whatever your situation, make sure you consider carefully this issue. If you contact me for becoming a student, please let me know what situation applies to you in your email.
  • Research Positions: these are for getting a job at the University of Sheffield. Postholders will become employees of the University of Sheffield and will receive a salary. Any employee of the University can study as a part time PhD while in post and no fees are due (However please consider that some positions require a PhD as entry requirement).
